Relive the magic

Thanks to the generosity of the bands and artists that performed, we've been able to put together a compilation of songs and music to help you relive that night. Hear some of your favorites again, or find something new you might have missed.

Keep on helping

Thanks to your donations, the barn benefit raised over $6000 to help towards Corey's medical bills.

Every donation that goes towards the purchase of the recording goes right into that fund and straight to Corey's family.
